Hostel

Boys Hostel

Boy’s hostel has an excellent facility that makes students feel comfortable as at home. Boys have their own mess. The mess is spacious with a dining hall and kitchen where the students are offered a choice of vegetarian and non- vegetarian food. The hostel is like an extended home place, conducive for students to groom and grow healthy. We have leisure time activities to make them excel intellectually, physically, psychologically and spiritually. Hostel has a playground to play in the evening hours and other sports activities are also encouraged to make them motivated and be focused.
